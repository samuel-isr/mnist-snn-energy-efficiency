
MNIST SNN vs CNN Results
========================

SNN Performance:
- Test Accuracy: 98.27%
- Train Accuracy: 98.73%
- Energy: 1.68 µJ
- Operations: 2.1e7

CNN Performance:  
- Test Accuracy: 98.90%
- Train Accuracy: 99.31%
- Energy: 31.45 µJ
- Operations: 8.5e8

Energy Efficiency:
- SNN achieves 18.7x energy reduction
- Operation reduction: 40.5x
- Accuracy trade-off: 0.63%

Conclusion:
SNNs are highly suitable for energy-constrained 
deployment scenarios with minimal accuracy loss.
